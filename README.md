# CS330-Portfolio - Jamisen Lilly
## How do I approach designing software?
When I approach designing software, I start by planning out what the program needs to do before writing any code. I focus on breaking the project into smaller pieces so it is easier to understand and manage. During this project, I learned that having a clear design before coding helps avoid confusion and reduces errors later.

## What new design skills has your work on the project helped you to craft?
This project helped me improve my ability to think about how objects interact in a system, especially in a 3D environment. I became more comfortable planning layouts, positioning objects, and thinking about how movement and interaction should work together.

## What design process did you follow for your project work?
I followed a step-by-step process where I first reviewed the requirements, then built the scene gradually by adding objects, textures, and lighting. I tested each part as I went to make sure everything worked correctly before moving forward.

## How could tactics from your design approach be applied in future work?
In future projects, I will continue to plan ahead and break problems into smaller steps. This approach helps me stay organized and makes debugging much easier when something goes wrong.

## How do I approach developing programs?
When developing programs, I focus on writing code in small sections and testing often. I try to keep my code simple and readable so it is easier to fix and improve later.

## What new development strategies did you use while working on your 3D scene?
I used more trial and error during this project, especially when working with positioning, lighting, and textures. I also relied on testing frequently to see how small changes affected the overall scene.

## How did iteration factor into your development?
Iteration was a big part of my development process. I made small changes, tested them, and then adjusted based on what I saw. This helped me improve the quality of my scene step by step.

## How has your approach to developing code evolved throughout the milestones?
At the beginning, I focused more on just getting things to work. As I progressed, I became more organized and started thinking more about structure, readability, and efficiency.
